Four points are placed for you. Drag the radius slider.

When two points are close enough, an edge forms between them — a 1-simplex. Connected points merge into one voice. β₀ decreases.

At small radius, four separate voices. As radius grows, components merge: four → three → two → one. The chord collapses into unison.

This is the Vietoris-Rips complex: connect all points within distance r of each other. The topology of this complex — its shape — determines the sound.
