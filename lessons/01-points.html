Points become sound

Overview Next →

Click anywhere above to place a point.

Each point is a 0-simplex — the simplest possible geometric object. On its own, it's an isolated voice.

Place more points. Each one adds an independent oscillator. The number of separate voices is β₀ — the zeroth Betti number. It counts connected components.

Right now, every point is its own component. Three points = three independent voices = a chord.

In the next lesson, we'll connect them.
