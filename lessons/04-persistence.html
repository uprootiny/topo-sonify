Drag the sweep slider slowly from left to right. Watch the persistence diagram build on the right.

Each dot in the diagram is a topological feature. Its position encodes when it was born (x-axis) and when it died (y-axis).

Points far from the diagonal lived a long time — they're persistent. Points near the diagonal were fleeting — probably noise. Distance from the diagonal = significance.

Purple dots are components merging (H₀). Red dots are loops appearing and filling (H₁). Each persistence pair becomes a delay tap in the audio — long-lived features echo longer.

Hit Auto sweep and Listen together. The filtration literally plays the topology.
